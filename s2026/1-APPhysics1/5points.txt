- Power = W/t = Fd/t = F*

- 2 Liquids in a U-tube -> Force is equal at bottom of top liquid

- F_N = apparent weight

- Atwood machine, F = (m1+m2)a

- Escape velocity can be derived from  infinite distance
	- E = U_g + K
	- 0 = -GMm/r + 1/2mv^2

- Slopes
	- x vs t -> velocity
	- v vs t -> accel
	- theta vs t -> angular velocity (omega)
	- w vs t -> angular accel (alpha)
	- delta p vs delta t -> Net Force
	- delta L vs delta t -> Net Torque

- Areas
	- v vs t -> Displacement
	- a vs t -> Change in v
	- w vs t -> Change in theta
	- alpha vs t -> Change in omega (angular velocity)
	- F_net vs t -> Impulse = Change in Momentum = Delta p
	- T vs t -> Angular Impulse = Change in Angular Momentum = Delta L
	- F_net vs x -> Work / Delta E
	- T_net vs theta -> Angular Work / delta E (this is most likely k_rot)

	- Satellite: L_sat = const & KE_sat = const

- Eliptical Orbit

	- Satellite: L_sat = constant & KE_g_toal NOTEQUAL const
