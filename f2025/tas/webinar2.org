*** Why a digging robot?

*** Computer ANimation
    - Land on lander, smaller commerical
    - Deploy on surface
    - Differnet deployment techniques based off of what lander
    - Drives path around lander and chooses ecavation lander
    - Not manually driven, full auto
    - Detect rocks and obstacles
    - Change path to correct dynamically
    - Estimate safe extraction area
    - Main focus is to excavate area in front of lander
    - Drives forward while drums spin, basically takes bite
    - Gets hot due to no air to dissapate heat
    - You have to actively pull heat away from electronics and dump it into empty space
    - Robot also has to recharge
    - Operating in dusty environment:
      - Avoid having to plug in robot becuase it could get dirty
      - Why no solar panels?, Dust covers them
      - Always recharge
    - Contactless charging, pretty awesome
    - Charge transfers through antennas



*** How robot works
    - Teeth on wheeels dig
    - Offset each tooth to minimize forces on robot
    - Can load collected material into hopper arm
    - Wheels can go over rocks
    - Arms and drums
    - Robot can change center of gravity by moving arms to gain wheel contact pressure -> climb steep slope
